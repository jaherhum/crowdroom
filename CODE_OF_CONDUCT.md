# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making Crowdroom a welcoming and respectful environment
for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender, nationality, or background.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy towards other contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainer at: crowdroom@jaherhum.dev

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Depending on severity:

1. **Warning** — a private written warning
2. **Temporary ban** — temporary restriction from commenting or contributing
3. **Permanent ban** — permanent removal from the project
